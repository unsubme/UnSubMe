Find Your iPhone Subscriptions

See every subscription connected to your Apple ID in less than 30 seconds.

How to Find Your Subscriptions

1

Open Settings

Tap the Settings app on your iPhone.

2

Tap Your Name

Your Apple Account appears at the very top.

3

Tap Subscriptions

This shows every active and expired subscription connected to your Apple ID.

4

Select Any Subscription

You can cancel, change plans, or renew directly from this screen.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where are subscriptions on iPhone?

Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions.

Why can't I find my subscription?

You may have subscribed directly through the company instead of Apple.

Does deleting an app cancel my subscription?

No. You must cancel it from the Subscriptions page.

Can I subscribe again later?

Yes. Most subscriptions can be restarted at any time.
