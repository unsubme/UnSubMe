You wanted the free trial. Life got busy. Let's see what you're still paying for.

STEP 1

Open Settings

Find the gray gear icon and tap it.

STEP 2

Tap Your Name

Your Apple Account is at the very top.

STEP 3

Tap Subscriptions

You'll see every active and expired subscription connected to your Apple ID.

STEP 4

Choose One

Tap any subscription to cancel, change, or renew it.
